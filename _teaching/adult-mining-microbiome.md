**Course Description:**  
This course introduces adult learners to the microbiome and its applications in food and health. It introduces natural competition within microbial ecosystems, decscribing the function of antimicrobial production and how researchers may exploit that to discover and novel antimicrobials or antimicrobial producing microbes.
